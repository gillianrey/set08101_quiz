Thanks for selecting the film quotes quiz landing page! Here you can find a bit more information about the quizzes before getting started!

The film quotes quiz's come in three different categories:

80's film quotes
90's film quotes
00's film quotes

These quizzes will provide TEN randomised questions over various genres from the decade selected. Once the quiz has started, you must continue until the end, or you can refresh the page to start over. At the end of the quiz you will receive your grade.

Please feel free to utilise the anonymous contact for any feedback, or you can e-mail directly using the link in the footer. Have fun and enjoy playing!
